It's one thing to travel, it's another thing to move to two different states and change your license around. 

## Growing up in Idaho

I grew up part of my life in Idaho, spending the majority of my childhood outdoors. Whether it was mowing my parents' lawn every Saturday morning, skateboarding the ramps I'd built since the town's skatepark only had a quarter pipe and a rail, or biking around town with friends, I was constantly on the move. Growing up in a household of brothers meant we were always outside running around together.
